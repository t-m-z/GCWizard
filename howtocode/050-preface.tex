\addchap{Preface}

When I started geocaching it took only a little time to notice that I need help to solve mysteries, decode or decrypt coordinates and so on. Hence I started to download apps and tables and searched for internet ressource. However, I missed always the one app, which replaces all.

The GCWizard I believe could be this one and only app. So I decided to contribute to reach this aim.

It took only some time and I started only with with coding baut with an steep learning curve. Within minutes I received overwhelming help from Mark 'S-Man42'. It was obvious, that his README.md at his github-repository was not sufficient.

Hence it was only a small step to collect all the pieces and bring them together.

Coding might be seen as an art. But after all it bases on knowledg, rules and guides.

I hope this living document ist helpful for other beeing interested in contributing but hesitating because of lacking insight.
